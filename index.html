TIC Industry - Case Study


Please open this page on a desktop or laptop.

The Testing, Inspection, and Certification (TIC) industry plays a critical role in ensuring the quality, safety, and compliance of products and services across various sectors, including mining, food, and environmental industries. TIC providers are essential in helping businesses meet regulatory standards and maintain global competitiveness through reliable testing, certification, and inspection services.
In this case study, we focus on analyzing the LinkedIn performance of a key player within the TIC industry. The selected time frame for this analysis is from 24th September 2023 to 22nd September 2024 (364 Days). Using a Tableau dashboard, we examine metrics such as Impressions, Engagement Rate, Organic Followers, and Total Followers to assess month-on-month growth. This analysis highlights the effectiveness of the company's LinkedIn strategy and how it overcame challenges to improve engagement, visibility, and raise awareness of its services within the TIC sector, particularly in the food and environmental markets.

The report and visualization were created by Poulami Mitra and Saikat Bhattacharjee.
