Mitra SK Case Study


Mitra SK is a leading provider of mineral testing, inspection, and certification services, specializing in ensuring the quality of bulk commodities. With a wide presence across multiple locations, Mitra SK serves industries such as mining, trading, food, environment, and industrial consumers, offering reliable testing and certification solutions. The company is committed to maintaining high standards of quality, compliance, and sustainability, helping its clients remain competitive in the global market through its trusted services.
In this case study, we focus on the LinkedIn page of Mitra SK, analyzing key social metrics. The selected time frame for this analysis is from 26th August to 24th September. Utilizing a Tableau dashboard, we aim to assess the month-on-month growth of LinkedIn metrics such as Impressions, Engagement Rate, Organic Followers, and Total Followers.
